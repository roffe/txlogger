txlogger – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-26

txlogger respects your privacy. This application does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information about you.

Information We Collect

txlogger does not collect any personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or other contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers linked to you as an individual

All processing related to the application’s functionality happens locally on your device.

Data Transmission

txlogger does not send any personal data to external servers or third-party services. All application activity and data processing occur locally on your device.

The only network communication performed by txlogger is described in the Update Checks section below.

Update Checks

txlogger may check for a newer version by requesting the latest release information from our publicly available (GitHub releases page).

When performing this check:

  • txlogger sends a simple request to retrieve the latest available version number.
  • No personal information, usage data, or ECU/vehicle data is sent.
  • The comparison between your installed version and the latest version happens locally.

If you prefer, you can answer no on the periodic update checks in the application and no network requests will be made.

Permissions and Diagnostic Data

txlogger may request access to system resources (such as file system access or network interfaces) solely to provide its core functionality, including:

  • Listening on 224.0.0.0:5353 (mDNS) to be able to discover txbridge devices on the local network
  • Reading diagnostic data from supported vehicle control units (ECUs) via supported CANBUS devices
  • Saving logs and configuration files locally on your device

Any diagnostic or log data handled by txlogger is processed and stored locally on your device. It is not automatically uploaded or transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

txlogger does not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Cloud storage for user data
  • Social media plugins

Children’s Privacy

txlogger does not collect personal information from any users, including children. As no personal data is collected, the application is suitable for users of all ages from a data-privacy perspective.

Your Rights under GDPR (EU/EEA)

txlogger is designed so that we do not collect or process personal data. In practice, this means:

  • We have nothing to access, correct, delete, or export about you.
  • We do not perform profiling or automated decision-making based on your personal data, as no such data is collected.

If you have questions about how txlogger operates with respect to privacy and data protection, you can contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page and, where appropriate, in the application listing.

If we ever introduce features that involve collecting or processing personal data, we will update this policy to describe those changes clearly.
